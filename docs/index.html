🔒 Privacy Policy

Loan Decision Helper – India

Last updated: July 2025

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

Overview

Loan Decision Helper is a financial calculator app designed to help users calculate EMIs, compare loans, and check affordability. The app runs entirely on your device with no server or cloud connection.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No financial data is transmitted off your device
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or identifiers
  • No location data

Local Storage

The app stores your saved calculations locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time from within the app
  • Is automatically removed when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising (ads are placeholder only), or tracking services.

Internet Permission

The app does not require internet access to function. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is a general-purpose financial calculator suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
